# Business AI Prompts
1. Brand Voice Persona
> "Analyze the following [website/text sample] and define our brand’s tone of voice. Create a set of brand guidelines including preferred vocabulary, tone (e.g., professional yet witty), and a list of 'never use' phrases."
> 
2. Content Calendar Strategy
> "Generate a 30-day content calendar for [Platform, e.g., LinkedIn] targeting [Ideal Customer Profile]. Focus on three pillars: educational value, industry authority, and soft-sell testimonials."
> 
3. Ad Copy Variations
> "Write five distinct versions of Facebook ad copy for [Product/Service]. Vary the hooks: one based on fear of missing out (FOMO), one on a specific pain point, one on a 'dream state' outcome, one short/punchy, and one long-form story."
> 
4. SEO Blog Outlining
> "Create a comprehensive SEO-optimized outline for a blog post titled '[Topic]'. Include H1-H3 headers, a list of suggested semantic keywords, and a compelling meta-description."
> 
Strategy & Operations
5. SWOT Analysis
> "Conduct a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) for a business in the [Industry] sector that specializes in [Specific Niche]."
> 
6. Competitor Gap Analysis
> "Based on these [Competitor Reviews/Descriptions], identify three gaps in their service that my business, which focuses on [My Strength], can exploit in its marketing."
> 
7. Business Model Canvas
> "Help me build a Business Model Canvas for a new [Type of Business]. Walk me through the 9 building blocks, starting with the Value Proposition and Customer Segments."
> 
8. Meeting Summarization
> "I’m going to paste a transcript of a meeting. Please extract the key decisions made, the specific action items assigned to individuals, and any unresolved questions for the next session."
> 
Sales & Customer Success
9. Cold Outreach Personalization
> "Write a three-step cold email sequence for [Target Job Title]. The goal is to book a 15-minute discovery call. Keep the first email under 75 words and focus on [Specific Problem] we solve."
> 
10. Handling Common Objections
> "Create a 'rebuttal playbook' for our sales team. Provide empathetic but persuasive responses to the top three objections we hear: 'It’s too expensive,' 'We’re already using a competitor,' and 'Now isn’t a good time.'"
> 
11. Customer Onboarding Checklist
> "Draft a high-touch onboarding checklist for a new client in a [SaaS/Service] business to ensure they see 'Time to Value' within the first 7 days."
> 
12. Personalized LinkedIn Outreach
> "Draft a LinkedIn connection request and a follow-up message for a potential partner in [Industry]. Make it feel human, non-salesy, and focused on mutual networking."
> 
Human Resources & Leadership
13. Job Description Crafting
> "Write a job description for a [Job Title] that balances technical requirements with our company culture of [Culture Values]. Focus on outcomes the person will achieve in their first 6 months rather than just a list of tasks."
> 
14. Performance Review Framework
> "Provide a template for a quarterly performance review that focuses on 'Radical Candor.' Include prompts for both positive reinforcement and constructive growth areas."
> 
15. Team Building Idea Generator
> "Suggest five remote-friendly team-building activities for a group of [Number] people that focus on improving [Specific Skill, e.g., creative problem solving] without being 'cringe'."
> 
Finance & Legal
16. Cash Flow Forecast Logic
> "Explain the formula for calculating 'Burn Rate' and 'Runway' for a startup. Then, create a simple Markdown table template I can use to track these monthly."
> 
17. Basic Contract Clause Explanation
> "Explain this [Clause Name, e.g., Indemnification] clause in plain English. What are the potential risks for me as the [Service Provider/Client]?"
> 
18. Negotiation Scripting
> "I am negotiating a contract with a vendor. They want [Price/Term], but I need [Price/Term]. Script a negotiation strategy using the 'principled negotiation' method."
> 
Product & Innovation
19. User Persona Development
> "Create two detailed user personas for a [Product Name]. Include their daily frustrations, their professional goals, and what specific feature of our product would make them 'the hero' at their company."
> 
20. Product Naming Brainstorm
> "Suggest 15 creative names for a new [Product Category] that evokes a sense of [Emotion, e.g., security/speed]. Categorize them into 'Literal,' 'Metaphorical,' and 'Modern/Abstract'."
